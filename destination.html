Oslob, Cebu

Oslob is quite far from the city proper of Cebu but several tourists go here to go swim and see the whale sharks. This place is known for the whale shark watching. It is an experience you don't get to have all the time.

El Nido, Palawan

El Nido is a small town in Palawan but has a lot of islands near it that you could tour. Make sure you'll allot time to visit some of these islands!

Calaguas Islands, Camarines Norte

Enjoy the white sand of Calaguas Islands which is located at Camarines Norte. You will also have fun with the crystal-clear water of this place.

Oslob, Cebu

Several tourists these days go to Cebu just to see the whale sharks, whether they know how to swim or not. For those who can't swim can choose to stay on the boat and you'll still see the whale sharks although it is not as up close as those who will swim.

There are different packages to see the whale sharks. There's the Whale shark watching and snorkeling, scuba diving with whale sharks, and many more.

After you enjoy watching the whale sharks, you might want to visit Tumalog falls because it will be on the way when you go back to the city proper.

For more information, just go to this website.

El Nido, Palawan

El Nido has different islands that you could go to via island hopping. There are many locals who offer tour packages. A day isn't enough to tour these places. If you plan to visit El Nido, I suggest stay for at least 4 days because less than that wouldn't be enough.

You can book a direct flight going here but it will be quite expensive. Some tourists choose to book a flight to Puerto Princesa then go to El Nido via riding a van. Just a reminder, the transportation by land will take 5 hours and the road isn't that developed yet.

There are few islands that if it's high tide, you can't visit them. The locals who will be with you during the tour will advise you if it's safe to go there.

Calaguas Islands, Camarines Norte

You have two options when going to Calaguas. One is to go there by land via bus and the other one is by air. You could book a flight to Naga, Camarines Sur then ride a van going to Daet. Daet is a two-hour drive from Naga.

Since the number of tourists going to Calaguas is increasing, there are now several cottages and resorts where you can stay.

There are activities you can enjoy while staying in Calaguas. You can also di island hopping and visit old churches in the neighboring towns.
